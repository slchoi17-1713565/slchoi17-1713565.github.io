What's Happening?

Shish keBOB only has two types of flavors -- bold and strong. This is a restaurant for those who must brave their weaknesses, for those who have goals, and for those who will be triumphant. Do you want the spice of life? Do you already have it? Join us. Here's a link to our menu.

Wednesday, Oct. 31st

This Halloween, trick or treat yourself so some easy dinner with our spoooky keBOBs!

We are so excited to have you join us for our special Halloween shingdig! From 5pm-6pm, we'll be giving out free keBOBs to everyone! Also for a limited time, if you refer a friend we'll give you coupon for 50% off your next purchase! Limit is one per person.

Saturday, Jan. 26th

New location in New York!

We are more than psyched to be opening our third location in New York City! Big city, Big Apple, Big mood. At all our participating locations, we will be serving free shish keBOBs to everyone who comes until supplies last! Tag us in your post and receive 20% off your next purchase.

Merchandise Paradise

New merch dropping November 23rd!

As of November 23rd, all Shish keBOB locations will be selling new Sk merchandise! Stay toasty with an Sk beanie or pompom hat! We also love us ultra-soft, organic cotton t-shirts, short and long-sleeved. Speaking of soft, we are also releasing keBOB plushies! Great gifts for anyone on your list!
