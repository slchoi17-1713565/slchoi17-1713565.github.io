The Beginning

It starts in the stomach

Creating and eating food is an art. This is what Shish keBOB is founded on. Shish keBOB is a place for everyone to enjoy. Since the beginning, our menu has included dairy-free, gluten-free, vegetarian, and vegan options. All our food are the results of decades of food theory, practice and perfection and we would serve nothing less. Our goal is to craft food that combines simplicity and beauty without sacrificing taste.
